Kristen & Charlie

Dear Expectant Parent(s),

We are Charlie and Kristen. We are so happy and thankful that you have decided to read our album. We hope that we can give you an understanding of who we are and the type of loving, nurturing life we can give your child. We have always wanted to be parents and cannot wait to start that journey.

We met in 2006 on Match.com and fell in love after a short time together. We are both very close to our families and shared a desire to start a family of our own once we were married. We also had many other things in common- including being from Michigan, graduating from Michigan State University, and sharing the same birthdate (we are one year apart).

We were married in 2008 and spent our honeymoon in France and Italy. In 2009, we bought our house in a northwestern suburb because of the great school district and close proximity to the elementary school and parks. We were drawn in by the family-oriented atmosphere, including annual block parties in our neighborhood. Although we established roots in Illinois, we enjoy visiting our families and friends in Michigan. We also have a little dog named Stella who loves the neighborhood children and our nieces and nephews.

One of our passions is traveling to new places and learning about local history and cultural traditions, and can't wait to share that experience with a child. We chose Italy for our honeymoon since Kristen's grandparents both emigrated from Italy to the U.S. We also love gardening, shopping at antique stores, skiing, attending sporting events, eating at new restaurants, and going to the theatre.

We both have successful careers and can provide a financially stable life for your child. We would love to have our child attend college, like we did, but will respect and encourage whatever path he or she chooses.

We understand that this may be a difficult time in your life, and we respect your decision to consider adoption. We have so much admiration for people who put someone else's needs before their own. We hope that you will consider us when making your decision.

Sincerely,

Charlie and Kristen

ABOUT US:

CHARLIE:

Charlie grew up in a small town in southwest Michigan. He is the oldest of three boys. His fondest memories of childhood are those that involve being outdoors with his brothers and riding a tractor, snowmobile, or dirt bike.

Charlie graduated from Michigan State University with a degree in Horticulture. He works as a project manager for a residential landscape company. He has a true passion for his work and loves sharing gardening tips with our neighbors.

In his free time, he enjoys skiing, gardening, exploring Chicago, trying new restaurants, woodworking, and visiting friends and family.

He is most excited about being able to share his favorite hobbies with a child and also being able to teach him/her new things.

KRISTEN:

Kristen grew up in a suburb of Metro Detroit. She has a large family that includes a sister, half-sister, half-brother, step-brother and step-sister. She is very close to her family and loves spending time with them. She grew up taking dance and competed in tap and jazz while in high school. She still enjoys staying active by taking Zumba.

She graduated from Michigan State University with a degree in Merchandising Management. She currently works as a Category Management Specialist at the headquarters for a large Chicago-based retailer.

In her free time, she loves baking and trying new recipes (Charlie loves to be the test subject!). She also enjoys traveling, shopping, going to the theater, gardening, and planning parties.

Kristen loves being around children (especially her nieces and nephews) and can't wait to be a mom!
